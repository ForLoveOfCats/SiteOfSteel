FAQ:

    How do I install a gamemode?
    Open SkyOfSteel and on the main menu press the "Open Data Folder" button. Once in the data folder, open the "Gamemodes" folder and copy the gamemode's folder into here. Then ingame you can press the tilda/grave key on your keyboard to open the console. With the console open simply type 'Gamemode("NameOfTheGamemodeHere")' to load the gamemode
